## üíº Key Projects (2024)

Here are three featured projects from my academic year, showcasing my journey and growing expertise in full-stack development.

### **CC School** | Semester 1 (Term 1 & 2)
[![CC School Preview](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/481a53e0-9d26-46ac-854a-afc0c376a833)](https://github.com/Mwape-Kurete/CC-School-Frontend)
A comprehensive school management system built to streamline administrative and academic processes.
*   **How:** Developed with a **Vue** frontend and a **C#/.NET** backend, using **MySQL** for data persistence.
*   **Why:** To solve our clients problem when it came to not having a digital or modern system for administrative load and admin, lecturer. and student communication.

---

### **Reverb** | Semester 2 (Term 3)
[![Reverb Preview](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/07e1331c-da89-4990-b93f-5442ab27bdc3)](https://github.com/Mwape-K/REVERB)
A unique audio journaling application built to make journaling a more interesting experience.
*   **How:** Built using the **React Native** & **Firebase** with a focus on a real-time, responsive UI.
*   **Why:** Traditional journaling (diaries etc.) works for some people but doesn't always *stick* for others, REVERB aims to solve this by introducing a new way to journal and connect your thoughts to moments & music.

---

### **THNK** | Semester 2 (Term 4)
[![THNK Preview](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e026b1e7-4617-4511-8aa1-f638516c6dde)](https://github.com/Mwape-Kurete/thnk-frontend)
A critical-thinking and research tool
*   **How:** Built with **React** and **Tailwind** for a fast, aesthetic and seamless inspired frontend, powered by a robust **MongoDB** backend.
*   **Why:** To address the need for deeper learning when researching topics or looking through sources.
